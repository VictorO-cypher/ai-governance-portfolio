# AI Governance Portfolio

## Project Description

This repository contains an AI Impact Assessment (AIIA) for TalentScreen AI, a fictional AI-powered resume screening system classified as a high-risk AI system under the EU AI Act. The assessment identifies potential risks to job applicants, evaluates their severity and likelihood, and proposes governance controls aligned with internationally recognized AI governance frameworks.

## Frameworks Applied

- NIST AI Risk Management Framework (Govern, Map, Measure, Manage)
- EU AI Act (High-Risk AI Systems – Annex III, Area 4)
- AI Impact Assessment methodology
- Risk Register methodology

## Repository Contents

- **ai-impact-assessment-talentscreen.pdf** – Final AI Impact Assessment document.
- **ai-impact-assessment-talentscreen.md** – Markdown version for viewing directly on GitHub.
- **risk-register-talentscreen.csv** – Structured AI risk register containing identified risks, risk scores, mitigation controls, owners, and implementation status.

## Frameworks Referenced

- NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF 1.0)
- EU AI Act (Regulation (EU) 2024/1689)
- ISO/IEC 42001 – Artificial Intelligence Management Systems
